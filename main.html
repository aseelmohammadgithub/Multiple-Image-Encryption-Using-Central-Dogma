Encrypting and decrypting images using chaotic functions harness the unpredictability of chaotic systems to secure digital data. Chaotic functions introduce complexity, making it challenging for unauthorized parties to decipher encrypted information.

This approach aligns with the central dogma of cryptography, emphasizing confidentiality, integrity, and authenticity in safeguarding sensitive data through the strategic application of mathematical chaos and cryptographic principles.
